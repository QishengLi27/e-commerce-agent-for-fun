Return Policy:
Our store offers a 30-day return window for most items. You can return products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition with all packaging and accessories. Electronics and certain high-value items may have a 14-day return period. To initiate a return, please contact our support team or use the online return portal on our website. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Shipping and Delivery:
We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard delivery typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited options are available for an additional fee. You can track your order status using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. If your order is delayed, please check the estimated delivery date in your account or contact support for updates.

Warranty Information:
All electronics come with a manufacturer's warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty period varies by product but is typically 1 year from the date of purchase. If you experience an issue covered by warranty, please contact the manufacturer directly or our support team for assistance. We do not offer extended warranties, but third-party options may be available.
